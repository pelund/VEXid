This is the rankings screen! Here, you can check what rank your team is in.

WP is a sum of the follwing results:

  • 2 points for a Win
  • 1 point for a tie
  • 0 points for a loss

AP is the amount of autnomouses won, times 4

SP is the sum of points won by the teams who lost in a match that team was involved in.

Teams are ranked by the highest score in the following categories:
  • WP
  • AP
  • SP
  • Highest Match Score
  • Coin Flip
